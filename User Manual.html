Mecanum Wheel Operation

The mecanum wheel is a type of omnidirectional wheel that allows the Arduino car to move in any direction with ease. It achieves this using a unique arrangement of rollers at a 45-degree angle to the axis of rotation.

When the wheels rotate at different speeds and directions, the car can achieve complex movements, including lateral and diagonal motions. This provides exceptional maneuverability, making the Arduino car highly versatile in navigating various environments.
